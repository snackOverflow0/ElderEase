# ElderEase

## Project Title

**ElderEase: Web-Based Senior Citizen Assistance and Benefits Management System**

---

# Project Description

ElderEase is a web-based information management system developed to help the Office of Senior Citizens Affairs (OSCA) manage senior citizen records, government benefits, appointments, and reports efficiently.

The system aims to replace manual record-keeping and paper-based processes with a centralized digital platform that improves productivity, data accuracy, and service delivery.

---

# Problem Statement

Many local government units (LGUs) still manage senior citizen information using paper records and spreadsheets. These manual processes often lead to:

- Lost or duplicated records
- Slow registration and record retrieval
- Difficulty tracking benefits
- Inefficient appointment scheduling
- Time-consuming report generation
- Increased workload for OSCA staff

As the number of senior citizens continues to grow, managing these records manually becomes increasingly inefficient.

---

# Proposed Solution

ElderEase provides a centralized web application where authorized personnel can securely manage senior citizen information, benefits, appointments, and reports.

The system reduces paperwork, improves data accuracy, and allows staff to access information quickly through an intuitive dashboard.

---

# Objectives

## General Objective

Develop a secure and user-friendly web-based management system that improves the efficiency of senior citizen services provided by OSCA.

- Digitize senior citizen records.

- Improve appointment scheduling.
- Generate reports automatically.
- Reduce paperwork.
- Improve record accuracy.

---

# Scope

The first version of ElderEase will include:

- User Authentication
- Dashboard
- Senior Citizen Registry
- Benefits Management
- Appointment Management
- Reports
- User Management

---

# Limitations

The system will not include:

- Mobile application
- Online payment integration
- SMS notifications
- Email notifications
- AI chatbot
- GIS mapping

These features may be considered in future versions.
